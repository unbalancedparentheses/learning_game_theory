# Learning Game Theory

Game theory, mechanism design, and strategic interaction.

## Books

### Introductory
- [An Introduction to Game Theory](https://www.amazon.com/Introduction-Game-Theory-Martin-Osborne/dp/0195128958) - Martin Osborne. Best first textbook, clean and rigorous.
- [Game Theory: An Introduction](https://www.amazon.com/Game-Theory-Introduction-Steven-Tadelis/dp/0691129088) - Steven Tadelis. More applied, good for economists.
- [The Art of Strategy](https://www.amazon.com/Art-Strategy-Theorists-Success-Business/dp/0393337170) - Dixit & Nalebuff. Non-technical, great for intuition.

### Intermediate and Advanced
- [Multiagent Systems: Algorithmic, Game-Theoretic, and Logical Foundations](http://www.masfoundations.org/) - Shoham & Leyton-Brown. Free online. Bridges CS and game theory.
- [Algorithmic Game Theory](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~sandholm/cs15-892F13/algorithmic-game-theory.pdf) - Nisan, Roughgarden, Tardos, Vazirani. The reference for computational game theory.
- [Game Theory](https://www.amazon.com/Game-Theory-Drew-Fudenberg/dp/0262061414) - Fudenberg & Tirole. Graduate-level, comprehensive.
- [A Course in Game Theory](https://www.amazon.com/Course-Game-Theory-Martin-Osborne/dp/0262650401) - Osborne & Rubinstein. Formal treatment.

### Mechanism Design
- [Auction Theory](https://www.amazon.com/Auction-Theory-Vijay-Krishna/dp/0123745071) - Vijay Krishna. The standard reference.
- [Mechanism Design: A Linear Programming Approach](https://www.amazon.com/Mechanism-Design-Programming-Econometric-Society/dp/1107004586) - Vohra.

### Evolutionary and Behavioral
- [Evolution and the Theory of Games](https://www.amazon.com/Evolution-Theory-Games-John-Maynard/dp/0521288843) - John Maynard Smith. The classic on evolutionary game theory.
- [Thinking Strategically](https://www.amazon.com/Thinking-Strategically-Competitive-Edge-Business/dp/0393310353) - Dixit & Nalebuff.

## Papers

### Foundational
- [Nash Equilibrium Points in N-Person Games](https://www.jstor.org/stable/88031) - Nash (1950). One page that changed everything.
- [The Market for Lemons](https://www.jstor.org/stable/1879431) - Akerlof (1970). Information asymmetry.
- [Optimal Auction Design](https://www.jstor.org/stable/2981014) - Myerson (1981). Revenue equivalence and optimal mechanisms.
- [Bayesian Games and Mechanism Design](https://econweb.ucsd.edu/~jsobel/172aw02/notes10.pdf) - Introduction to Bayesian game theory.

### Computational and Algorithmic
- [Algorithmic Mechanism Design](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/301250.301287) - Nisan & Ronen (1999). Where CS meets mechanism design.
- [Combinatorial Auctions](https://www.amazon.com/Combinatorial-Auctions-Peter-Cramton/dp/0262033429) - Cramton, Shoham, Steinberg.
- [The Complexity of Computing a Nash Equilibrium](https://people.csail.mit.edu/costis/simplified.pdf) - Daskalakis, Goldberg, Papadimitriou (2009). PPAD-completeness.

### Cryptoeconomics and Protocol Design
- [Flash Boys 2.0: Frontrunning, Transaction Reordering, and Consensus Instability](https://arxiv.org/abs/1904.05234) - Daian et al. (2019). MEV.
- [Credible Decentralized Exchange Design via Verifiable Sequencing Rules](https://arxiv.org/abs/2209.15569) - Ferreira & Parkes (2022).
- [Foundations of Cryptoeconomics](https://assets.pubpub.org/aysokl8j/31581339882872.pdf) - Overview of incentive design in protocols.
- [SoK: MEV Countermeasures](https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.05111) - Heimbach et al.

### Social Choice
- [Arrow's Impossibility Theorem](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/arrows-theorem/) - Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy overview.
- [Gibbard-Satterthwaite Theorem](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gibbard%E2%80%93Satterthwaite_theorem) - Strategy-proofness impossibility.

## Applications to Distributed Systems

- Consensus as a game-theoretic problem
- Token mechanism design and incentive compatibility
- Schelling points and focal points in coordination games
- VCG mechanisms in decentralized protocols
- MEV and transaction ordering games
